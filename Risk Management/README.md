# 11 - Risk Management 📊

## Overview
Risk management involves identifying, assessing, and prioritizing
risks to organizational assets and implementing controls to mitigate them.

## Labs in this folder

| Lab | Description |
|---|---|
| [Risk Register](./risk-register.md) | Bank risk assessment with likelihood and severity scoring |
| [Home Asset Inventory](./home-asset-inventory.md) | Asset classification and sensitivity assessment |

## Skills Covered
- Risk register creation
- Likelihood and severity scoring
- Risk prioritization matrix
- Asset inventory and classification
- Sensitivity level assignment
